CIA TRIAD 

Hello everyone. Aniket Paul this side. Today i will discuss the CIA TRIAD concepts in below :

The aspect of CIA TRIAD is a one of the most important thing in the concern of cybersecurity, the aspect of CIA TRIAD is divided into three form, which is as follows :

        1. C = Confidentiality ;
        2. I = Integrity ;
        3. A = Availability ;

1. CONFIDENTIALITY :
Keep information secret.
Only authorized people should be able to see the data.

        Example:
          Your email password;
          Your bank account details;

        How it is protected:
              Passwords, 
              Encryption, 
              Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA),

Question to ask:
Who can see the data?

2. INTIGRITY :
Keep information correct and unchanged.
Data should not be modified or damaged by unauthorized people.

        Example:

        If your bank balance is $1,000, nobody should be able to change it to $10,000 or $100.

        How it is protected:
        Hashing;
        Digital signatures;
        Checksums;

Question to ask:
Has the data been changed?

3. AVAILABILITY :
Make sure information and systems are available when needed.

        Example:

        You should be able to access your online banking app whenever you need it.

        How it is protected:
        Backups;
        Power supply (UPS);
        Redundant systems;
        DDoS protection;

Question to ask:
Can users access the data when they need it?

There is an example by which you are understand the working functions of the aspect CIA TRIAD. 

EXAMPLE :

  Confidentiality: Only you can see your account details.
  
  Integrity: Your balance cannot be changed by hackers.
  
  Availability: The banking website should work whenever you need it.
